About Us

We are a small time, family-owned pet store located in Urbana, IL. Though we do have all types of pets, we mainly specialize in cats and cat goods. Our friendly, trained staff are experts in pet care and will be able to answer any and all questions you may have about owning or taking care of your pet. Our address is 201 N. Goodwin Ave. Urbana, IL 61801.
